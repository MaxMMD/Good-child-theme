The Good Agency Elementor Child Theme is a simple child theme developed to work with the [Hello Elementor](https://wordpress.org/themes/hello-elementor/) theme.

***Good Agency Elementor Child*** is distributed under the terms of the GNU GPL v3 or later.

== Description ==

Crafted by Good Agency, this child theme is streamlined and efficient, perfect for integrating with the Elementor page builder.

By implementing this theme, the WordPress environment is optimized for the flawless functionality of Elementor.

== Installation ==

1. In your admin panel, go to Appearance > Themes and click the 'Add New' button.
2. Click 'Upload theme' and upload the zipped child.
3. Click on the 'Activate' button to apply your new theme immediately.
4. Dive into Elementor and commence your site's construction.

== Customizations ==

Most users won't need to delve into the files for this theme's customization.
For aesthetic adjustments to your site, it's recommended to utilize ***Elementor***.

For those who need a deeper level of customization, further instructions are available.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

**Is this theme compatible with other plugins?**

The Good Agency Elementor Child Theme is primarily designed for Elementor but does support WooCommerce.

**Is it possible to modify Font Styles via the theme's CSS?**

Certainly, ***however***, the ideal method remains utilizing the stylistic tools within the Elementor plugin.
